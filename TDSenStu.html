Welcome to the Senior Student Menu

This aims to serve as both a reference and guide for additional privileges, responsibilities, and expectations granted as part of being a senior student or intern.

Expectations

As part of this promotion, you now have additional responsibilities and expectations for how you perform your job.

As a Senior Student or Intern you now more greatly represent the Tech Desk, ITS, and all of its departments. In addition to serving as a model within ITS and the Tech Desk you now need to be able to provide even greater client facing service, this includes over email and tickets, but in person as well.

Because we often interact with people more than other departments our ability to be efficient and friendly serves as an opportunity to gain trust among the Students, Faculty, and Staff at the University of St. Thomas, this enables us all to better help those around us. 

General Expectations can be found in this article toward the bottom. Please remember that this role implies you can perform the standard and fundamental expectations for a typical student, it is now expected you do them to a greater degree in addition to performing your new responsibilities. It is also expected that you now help your fellow co-workers within the tech desk and can communicate with them about issues and things that can be improved in a polite and effective manner.

Below you can find articles relating to the new permissions gained with your new role as well as articles you need to know, please review, or study them. For the review articles it is expected you know the material, or at least are aware they exist so you know where to reference them. If you have any questions regarding the material, please ask.

Congratulations.
